OpenIrekia-Android

Mobile Android client for the Basque Government's oGov platform

As part of the Open Irekia project, the Basque Government has released under the EUPL bits of free software. This is an unofficial mirror of the software releases for the Android mobile client. See the github repo. Or download the app for Android.
